Step 3
Vanessa
CS Team
Requisition Sorting (~2–3 hrs/day)
Manual sort of paper requisitions by payment type: Cash & Card / Bill Client / Toxicology / Insurance / SNF. Labels applied. Problem cases logged. LIS corrections made if new info is obtained.
Problem DM log ~2–3 hrs daily bottleneck
↓
Step 4
Annabel
Billing Team
Reconciliation — Core Data Transformation
Master DM → Reconciliation sheet. Fields enriched via VLOOKUP from external reference files (Rep List, TEST LIST). Non-billable and special cases separated into sub-sheets. Culture CPT codes entered manually — most time-intensive step.
+ PECOS + REP + BILLING TYPE + TEST TYPES + CULTURES NEW CODES + DIAGNOSIS_13–20 − QA-only rows − PANEL_CODE STAT / Cultures / Send-out → sub-sheets
↓
Step 5
Ashley
+ Annabel
QA Review — Verification & Escalation
Secondary review: address, NPI/PECOS, insurance, secondary insurance, diagnosis codes, STAT & Culture flags. Problems escalated to Missing REQ log or NoINS log. Scan log maintained to confirm all requisitions accounted for.
+ STAT TEST flag + CULTURES flag & CPT + STATUS / NOTES − CPT_CODES − PANEL_STAT
↓
Step 6
Annabel
Ashley
Final Manifest — Biller-Ready Output
All internal fields stripped. Only externally required fields remain. Attached with Scanned Requisition (PDF) and LABDAQ Billing Report (PDF) for Insurance; QuickBooks Invoice (PDF) for Bill Client.
− All internal codes & flags − SSN, DOCTOR_CODE, REQ_INITIALS − BILLING TYPE, PECOS, QA notes + Scanned Req (PDF) + Billing Report (PDF)
↓
Step 7
Split by
Billing Type
Billing Type Split & Transmission
Insurance
Manifest + Req Scan + Billing Report → Synaps (current) / PRG (transitioning). Portal access currently held by Synaps.
Bill Client
Manifest + QuickBooks Invoice → direct facility billing. Monthly. Pricing varies by client (Medicare / % Medicare / custom).
Cash / Card
Cash Log only (Maria). No biller submission. EFT downloaded daily from bank.
↓
Step 8
Maria
+ Mina
AR Tracking & Payment Posting
Insurance EFT/checks forwarded to billers for EOB breakdown. Bill Client payments posted in QuickBooks. AR Aging reports maintained. Historical logs kept for dispute resolution.
